Drone Technologies

EMBEDDED_369 delivers cutting-edge Drone Technologies designed to serve the growing needs of industrial, commercial, and research sectors. We specialize in developing Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) equipped for applications such as aerial mapping, precision surveillance, environmental monitoring, and agricultural analysis. Our expert team designs custom drone systems integrated with advanced navigation, GPS mapping, obstacle avoidance, and high-resolution imaging capabilities to meet a wide range of operational requirements.

Our drone solutions are built to handle tasks in challenging environments, providing reliable performance in remote terrains, hazardous zones, and large-scale industrial facilities. We offer specialized services in aerial photogrammetry, land surveying, 3D mapping, and live event coverage. Additionally, our drones can be tailored for agriculture, assisting farmers with crop health monitoring, pest detection, and yield analysis through multispectral and thermal imaging. Each UAV is designed for easy control via ground stations or mobile apps, with options for autonomous and semi-autonomous flight.
