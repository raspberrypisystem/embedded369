About EMBEDDED_369

Our Story

Founded in 2024, EMBEDDED_369 has been at the forefront of technological innovation, specializing in robotics, automation, IoT, and AI-driven solutions. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create intelligent technologies that enhance human potential and revolutionize industries.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in scientific research, automation, and smart systems, inspiring a generation of creators building a smarter, sustainable world.

We envision a future where technology is accessible, intelligent, and environmentally responsible — driving progress and harmony across communities and industries.

Our Mission

To deliver cutting-edge embedded and AI-based solutions that empower industries, enhance education, and foster innovation through real-world applications.

We aim to bridge the gap between theory and practice by creating systems that solve real-life problems. Our mission extends to inspiring young minds through workshops, research, and hands-on learning.
