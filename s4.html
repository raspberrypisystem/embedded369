Project Innovation center

The Project Innovation Center at EMBEDDED_369 is a collaborative hub where students, startups, researchers, and corporate teams come together to turn visionary ideas into reality. Our center provides a fully equipped environment designed for rapid prototyping, technology validation, and product development. It’s an ideal platform for aspiring innovators to experiment with advanced tools, work with mentors, and build working prototypes that address real-world challenges in sectors such as robotics, automation, IoT, AI, and embedded systems.

The center offers access to state-of-the-art facilities, including development labs, 3D printing stations, electronic component libraries, and testing setups. We encourage interdisciplinary collaboration, bringing together experts from hardware, software, mechanical, and electronics backgrounds to jointly work on advanced projects. Our Innovation Center also hosts technical workshops, hackathons, seminars, and hands-on training programs to keep innovators updated with the latest trends and technologies in the industry.
